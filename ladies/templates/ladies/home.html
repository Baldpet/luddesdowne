Luddesdowne Ladies

Luddesdowne Ladies began in 2019 as a group of friends and family keen to start playing cricket. Luddesdowne Ladies has since grown into a close-knit team who's cricket abilities and love for the game continue to grow.

Softball cricket is the perfect game for those of all abilities with all kit provided! We currently play in the Kent Womens Softball Cricket League and are attending a number of festivals throughout the summer.

Training is held weekly at our ground, run by our two ECB Level 1 Coaches - Robyn and Matt. We are currently welcoming new players of all abilities, so please get in touch!

A Message from the Skipper

Luddesdowne Ladies Softball Cricket team has come a long way since the start. Cricket ability, skill and team spirit have, and continue to, increase. Highlights from the last year include coming third in the Kent Womens Softball Final 2021, as well as, hosting our first softball cricket festival! If you'd like to join our team, we are always looking for new players and would love to hear from you!
