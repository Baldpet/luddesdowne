Luddesdowne Mens

We have two teams competing in the Saturday Kent County Village League within Division 4 and 6a. In addition to league cricket, we also have a Sunday friendly XI. Friendships built throughout the years with local clubs have created longstanding friendly fixtures.

In the summer you can usually find us training at our ground on a Thursday night. Men and women are both welcome to play, so please come along if you are interested!
